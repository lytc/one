Selectors

One selector wrap "querySelectorAll" to support more features like: Adjacent previous sibling combinator ("-"), General previous sibling combinator ("<~")

$('#container').find('- div') //Adjacent previous sibling combinator
$('#container').find('<~ div') //General previous sibling combinator
